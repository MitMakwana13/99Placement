# TalentLab RMS - Client / Vendor Manual

## Welcome to the Client Portal
TalentLab allows your recruitment partners to share candidate profiles with you securely and efficiently.

## Reviewing Candidates
When an agency shares a candidate shortlist with you, you will receive a secure share link (e.g., `https://talentlab.co/share/c/...`).
1. **Access the Link**: No login is required. The link is secured by a unique cryptographic token.
2. **View Profiles**: You can review the candidate's resume, assessment scores, and AI-generated screening summaries.
3. **Provide Feedback**: 
   - Click **Shortlist** to move the candidate forward.
   - Click **Reject** to decline the candidate.
   - You can also leave private notes that sync directly to the recruiter's CRM pipeline.

## Security
Share links expire automatically based on the recruiter's settings (typically 7 to 30 days) or can be revoked manually by the agency. Your feedback is strictly confidential between you and the agency.
